WHAT IS PÂTISSERIE?

Pâtisserie is used to describe French pastries and the pastry they are sold in. Although the word is used quite liberally in English-speaking countries, in France and Belgium the law restricts its use to bakeries who employ licensed maître pâtissier (master pastry chefs).
